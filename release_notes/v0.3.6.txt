Fujiyama Renderer v0.3.6(Alpha) Release Notes
=============================================

New features since v0.3.5
-------------------------
 * Switched to C++11 to utilize standard thread library.
 * No more OpenMP dependency.

Features under development
--------------------------
 * Deformation motion blur
 * AOV support
 * Displacement shader
 * Subdivision surface
 * Deep shadow map
 * Alembic support
 * OpenVDB support
